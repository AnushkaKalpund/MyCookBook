Aloo Tikki

To taste happiness with MyCookBook

INGREDIENTS
  • Potatoes
  • Green peas
  • Chopped Coriander
  • Salt
  • chilli powder
  • chaat masala
  • lemon juice
  • Oil
DIRECTION !!!
  • Step 1:
      • Boil all the potatoes
  • Step 2:
      • In the boiled potatoes, add green peas, chopped coriander, chaat masala, chilli powder, salt and little bit of lemon juice!
  • Step 3:
      • Make small rounds of the mixture and fry them in oil
  • Step 4:
      • Serve with tomato ketchup

    Aloo Tikki is ready!
