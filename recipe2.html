Mixed Berries and Banana Smoothie

To taste happiness with MyCookBook

If you have got just 5 minutes and you have got time to blend up this nutrient packed, 5-ingredient breakfast or snack smoothie!!
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 3/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup low fat vanilla yogurt
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 teaspoon honey
    • Toppings for the smoothie
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Chia seeds
ITS TIME FOR THE PROCESS !!!
  • For smoothie:
      • Combine the berries, orange juice, yogurt, banana, and honey. In a blender and puree until smooth.
  • For the Toppings :
      • Pour the yumme smoothie in the bowl. Top with the Blueberries, raspberries with the chia seeds !

    The yummiest drinkable snack the smoothie is ready!!!!
